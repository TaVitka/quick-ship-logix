Types of Vehicles in Our Fleet

  • Trucks

    Our fleet includes a variety of trucks, ranging from small to large, capable of transporting different cargo sizes and weights. Equipped with advanced tracking technology, our trucks ensure real-time monitoring and secure transportation of goods.

  • Vans

    For smaller shipments and last-mile deliveries, our fleet includes vans equipped with efficient loading and unloading capabilities. These vehicles are ideal for urban deliveries and navigating narrow streets, ensuring timely and hassle-free transportation.

  • Trailers

    We offer a range of trailers, including dry vans, flatbeds, and refrigerated trailers, suitable for transporting various types of goods, including dry goods, oversized items, and temperature-sensitive products. Our trailers are equipped with modern amenities and safety features to safeguard cargo during transit.

  • Specialized Equipment

    In addition to standard vehicles, Quick Ship Logix maintains specialized equipment such as liftgate trucks, temperature-controlled units, and hazmat-certified vehicles to meet unique transportation requirements. These specialized vehicles ensure the safe and secure transportation of delicate or hazardous cargo.
