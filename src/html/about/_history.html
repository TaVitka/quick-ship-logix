Growth & Expansion

  • 2005

    Quick Ship Logix is established, specializing in time-sensitive shipping solutions to meet urgent demands.

  • 2010

    The company experiences rapid growth, expanding its footprint nationally by establishing strategically located distribution centers across key regions.

  • 2015

    Quick Ship Logix pioneers the integration of cutting-edge tracking technology, providing customers with real-time visibility into their shipments.

  • 2018

    Strategic collaborations are forged with leading carriers, augmenting service capabilities and ensuring seamless operations.

  • 2022

    Recognizing the global demand for expedited logistics, Quick Ship Logix ventures into international shipping, enabling businesses to reach markets worldwide.
