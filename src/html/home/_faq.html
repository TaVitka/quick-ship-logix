FAQ

  • How can I contact Interiors by Designers?

    You can contact us via email at info@interiorsbydesigners.com. Additionally, you can use the contact form on our website to send us a message directly.

  • What are your business hours?

    We are open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM local time. We are closed on weekends and major holidays.

  • Do you offer virtual consultations?

    Yes, we offer virtual consultations for clients who are unable to meet in person. Please contact us to schedule an appointment.

  • How long will it take to receive a response to my inquiry?

    We strive to respond to all inquiries within 1-2 business days. If you haven't heard back from us within this time frame, please check your spam folder or contact us again.

  • Can I visit your showroom to see samples of your work?

    Yes, we welcome visitors to our showroom by appointment. Please contact us to schedule a visit, and we'll be happy to show you examples of our work.

  • Do you offer design services for residential and commercial projects?

    Yes, we provide interior design services for both residential and commercial projects. Whether you're renovating your home or redesigning your office space, our team is here to help.
